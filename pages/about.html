Hi, friend! I'm so glad you stopped by today! I'm John Omar Esguerra, aka Omar and I'm a hopeless Craft Addict. Welcome to Craftaholic where you can find all sorts of craft tutorials and creative ideas for your home, kids, and holidays. Happy crafting!


I am a lover of art, foods, books, and all things creative. I am also passionate about Jesus and my family. I'm not a genius but just a student striving hard - studying hard. And I am also an Addict, not to drugs but to Crafts. That is why the title of my blog is "Craftaholic" meaning addicted to crafts.

I am studying Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Cagayan State University - Carig Campus. Specialty in Programming and Web Design and Development.

Here at Craftaholic, I share a variety of project types including home decor, fashion, recyclable materials, and holiday ideas. My goal is to prove that none of us are perfect; we can be creative and awesome while still being totally human and making mistakes. I am happiest when creating something and enjoy making all kinds of projects. And, I'm so glad you're along for the journey.
