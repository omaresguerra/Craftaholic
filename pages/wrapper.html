DIY: HOLIDAY HANDMADE WRAPPING PAPER
By: Omar Esguerra

HOLIDAY HANDMADE WRAPPING PAPER

Whether you're making your holiday gifts or buying them, make your loved ones feel extra special this year by creating your own DIY wrapping paper! Dressing up your gifts with handmade wrapping paper is such a great way to show how much you care. I'm excited to share it with you all today!

For this DIY project, I've used paper doilies as my crafting tool of choice. Use colorful paint to stamp on your designs and top it off with glitter for some extra sparkle. So grab some paper doilies from your kitchen and let's get started!

MATERIALS:
  • roll of craft paper
  • paper doilies
  • 2 colors of glitter paint (acrylic paint will work too)
  • glitter glue
  • small container
  • foam brush
STEP 1: Lay out craft paper on a flat surface. Cut to desired size and place a doily on top.

STEP 2: Pour the first color of paint in a small container. Dip your brush in and lightly dab over the open holes of the doily, using it as a stencil.

STEP 3: Peel back the doily to reveal your pattern.

STEP 4: Continue to randomly place the doily on the paper and paint until most of it is covered.

STEP 5: To add an accent color, grab a new doily along with your second color of paint and continue painting until you've reached your desired look.

STEP 6: Add dots of glitter glue to add dimension and extra sparkle.

I'm all about it. Easy, cute, and ridiculously cheap to make…my kind of crafting! Stay tuned to see some of the other fun crafts I've made !

Happy Crafting!
